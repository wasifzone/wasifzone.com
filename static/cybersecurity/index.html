Introduction to Cybersecurity

Hello! My name is Wasif Khan, and I created this page to share some important basics about cybersecurity.

Cybersecurity is all about protecting computers, networks, and digital data from unauthorized access, theft, or damage. In today's world, almost everything we do is online — from banking to social media — so understanding cybersecurity is very important.

Some key areas in cybersecurity include:

  • Passwords and Authentication: Always use strong, unique passwords. Multi-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security.
  • Protecting Personal Data: Avoid sharing sensitive information online. Use encrypted services whenever possible.
  • Safe Browsing: Be careful of suspicious links or websites. They might contain malware that can harm your computer.
  • Regular Updates: Keep your operating system and apps updated to protect against new vulnerabilities.
  • Awareness: Learn to recognize phishing emails, scams, and other cyber threats.

Cybersecurity is not only for experts — everyone, including students and beginners, can practice safe online habits. By understanding the basics, you can protect yourself and your data from hackers and cyber attacks.

This page is part of my website, and I hope it helps you understand the importance of cybersecurity. Keep learning, stay safe online, and remember that even small steps, like using strong passwords, make a big difference.
